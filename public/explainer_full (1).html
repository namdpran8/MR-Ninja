The Problem — What Enterprises Actually Feel

Duo is powerful.
But it's costing you more than the license.

The seat price is the smallest part of the cost. The real financial pain is developer time wasted wrestling with context limitations, manual prompt engineering, and zero visibility into whether AI is actually helping.

Problem 01

Context Limits Kill Large Reviews

GitLab Duo truncates at ~150k tokens. Enterprise MRs routinely run 5-10× that. Security files get silently skipped. Nobody knows what the AI missed.

Problem 02

20% of Dev Time on Prompt Engineering

Before every Duo invocation, developers spend 10-15 minutes manually linking issues, epic descriptions, API docs, and security policies — just so the AI has enough context to give a useful answer.

Problem 03

Nobody Can Prove AI is Working

Finance asks: "Is the Duo license worth it?" Engineering can't answer. There's no automated measurement of whether AI-assisted MRs actually ship faster than manual ones.
